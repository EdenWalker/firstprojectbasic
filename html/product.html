Dagon: Dungeon Evolved

Welcome to the realm of Dagon, where dungeons have evolved into living entities. Players are presented with a unique choice between two sides: Adventurer - Embrace the traditional role of an adventurer exploring dungeons.

- Experience a fresh perspective by becoming part of the dungeon environment.

Dungeon - Step into the shoes of a dungeon, embodying the Environment itself.
- Craft intricate mazes and assemble a clandestine army to defend your domain.
- Challenge the status quo of typical player versus environment and player versus player dynamics.
- Engage in the innovative EvE (Environment vs. Environment) and EvP (Environment vs. Player) gameplay.

Dive into the world of Dagon and choose your path - whether to venture as an intrepid explorer or to wield the powers of the environment as a dungeon master. Experience a gaming experience that blurs the lines between traditional roles and offers a new dimension of gameplay in the form of EvE and EvP encounters.

Grandsmith Art Of Forging



In a world blending industrial and medieval eras, Marcus is the son of a legendary blacksmith known as the Grandsmith. As the story unfolds, readers witness Marcus's journey as he learns the intricate art of blacksmithing from the ground up under his father's tutelage. The narrative promises to delve deep into the technical aspects of blacksmithing, offering readers a detailed exploration of the craft.

As the tale progresses, magic swords will make an appearance, adding a fantastical element to the story. The techniques showcased in the narrative are inspired by Japanese forging traditions, while the naming conventions draw from Chinese influences. The fusion of these cultural elements may present a unique experience for readers, especially with the intricate naming system.

With Japan renowned for its extensive research on the forging of weapons and armor, Marcus's story is poised to blend historical accuracy with fantastical elements, promising a rich tapestry of craftsmanship and magic in a world where industrial advancements meet medieval traditions.

[Uprising]



In the era of VRMMORPG, game developers raced to create captivating games. Five years into the VRMMORPG era, [Uprising] captivated the world with its unique concept of rebirth and the opportunity for godhood. While the idea of achieving godhood is common in games, [Uprising] introduced a novel twist by assigning it a monetary value. Players could obtain a stake in the company and influence the game world.

To experience a rebirth in [Uprising], players needed to fulfill a set of hidden prerequisites, which would then prompt them for a chance at rebirth. Each rebirth granted access to higher-tier jobs, ranks, or secret bonuses. Within one month of the game's launch, the first player achieved rebirth, unlocking a new race. Six months later, another player fulfilled the prerequisites to be reborn as nobility. As the game progressed, hidden classes emerged, yet the ultimate goal of godhood remained elusive.

Follow Marcus (MC) as he strives to become the first player to attain godhood or at least take the initial steps toward it. Could he become a demigod of... cooking? While the story is currently on hiatus, the author expressed interest in resuming it based on reader feedback. If there is sufficient interest, the tale of Marcus's journey may continue.
